Every purchase is a choice, but when you base your purchasing choice on just the price you can miss out on other critical factors. Quality is the most common one and now it's typical on almost all online shopping platforms to find reviews next to every item to help shoppers not only make their purchasing decision based on the price of the item, but also the quality of the item.

The 5 Leaf Rating indicates that a product will not only help you spend less money but will also result in less air polution, less clean water used, less energy used, and less impact on the land over it's longer lifespan than the conventional alternative.
